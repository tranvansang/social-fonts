Brand Description

About The Brand

Vevo is an American multinational video hosting service. It was founded on December 8, 2009 and is based in New York City. Vevo posts music videos from Sony Music Entertainment and Universal Music Group on its website and YouTube.

What font does Vevo use?

Vevo has had three different logos which all have used different fonts. Vevo's newest logo uses the font Steagal Bold. The only letter that's different is the "e" which is a custom design. You can view, test with the font generator, and download the font here.

From 2013 - 2016 Vevo's logo used a custom font. The font that's most similar to it is Cocon Bold.

Vevo's first logo, which has been obsolete since 2013 uses a font called Eurostile Lt Std Bold Extended 2.
