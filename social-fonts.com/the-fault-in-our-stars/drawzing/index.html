Brand Description

About The Brand

The Fault in Our Stars is John Green's sixth novel. It was published January 10, 2012. The story is narrated by a 16 year old girl with cancer named Hazel Grace Lancaster. Hazel's parents force her to attend a support group where she meets and falls in love with Augustus Waters, a 17 year old ex-basketball player who is also an amputee.

What font does The Fault in Our Stars use?

The Fault in Our Stars has a custom designed logo, however the fonts Crayon Crumble, Drawzing, Ds Eraser Cyr, Eraser Dust, and Eraser Regular are very similar to it. You can view, test with the font generator, and download the fonts on any of the links above.
