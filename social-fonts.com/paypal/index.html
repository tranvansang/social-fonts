About The Brand

PayPal is an American organization operating a worldwide online payments system that supports online money exchanges and serves as an electronic alternative to conventional paper methods like checks and money orders. PayPal is one of the world's biggest Internet payment companies. The organization works as a payment processor for online sellers, auction sites and other commercial clients, for which it charges a fee.

What font does PayPal use?

Paypal's most recent logo uses a font called Futura Bold Oblique. The only difference is PayPal's logo has this font's letters rounded. You can view, test with the font generator, and download the font here.

PayPal's previous logo used a font called Verdana Bold Italic.
